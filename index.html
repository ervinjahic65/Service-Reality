Who we are?

InCorpora is a dynamic business advisory, corporate services, and accounting firm. We add value and aim to support your needs and priorities with advice and solutions that support your goals. It’s not just ticking the regulatory boxes. Our customers include both foreign and local businesses, expatriates, entrepreneurs, private individuals, and high-net-worth families from 26 different countries.

Key areas of Expertise

Bank account opening

Our pre-screening is fast and efficient. Successful validation means we can open an IBAN account within days, even for the most complicated corporate structures.

Tax advice in Estonia and EU

Experienced in both Estonian and EU regulatory landscape. We make sure that your tax strategy serves your corporate goals, while being tax efficient and compliant.

Economical Substance

Substance is the key. We will set up your Estonian company with a suitable structure to meet your business needs and keep you out of trouble. International tax issues are written in our DNA.

FACTA, CRS, BEPS, DAC-6, and EU Tax Directives

We advise on your business and tax implications of FATCA, CRS, OECD BEPS, DAC-6, and EU Tax Directives.

Your Trusted Advisor

InCorpora is a trusted advisor to resident and non-resident customers. It’s our job to make sure your company administration is smooth, easy, and clear. Our business is to simplify the processes focusing on what matters and works best, and how to make it work for you. We are building long-lasting relationships aiming for a partnership where our advice and support is based on mutual trust, respect and understanding.

That’s our commitment, and our great value to you.

Why choose InCorpora?

InCorpora is authorised by the Estonian FIU (Financial Intelligence Unit) and licensed to provide financial, Trust and corporate services, including corporate structuring, advice on cross-border operations, and regulatory compliance support. We provide you with all the needed support services (including VAT registration, accounting and bookkeeping, payroll, tax advice, assistance in bank account opening etc), taking care of all the paperwork, and making your business setup as simple as possible.

All companies and corporate structures administrated by InCorpora are regarded as trustworthy in the eyes of the State, EU, and The Banks. Making money utilising Estonia’s digital powerhouse has tax related and other benefits. Yet, regulatory and tax compliance is important. We’ve developed and run a state-of-the-art standard for efficient compliance processes making things easy for you. Our pre-screening systems help you validate if your company meets all the criteria for required substance, tax compliance and opening a bank account.
